About Me

I am a Software Developer with most of my experience in Game Development. I have the most experience in Physics and Artificial Intelligence programming. My strongest language is C++, and I've also worked with C, JavaScript, C#, Lua, and HTML.

I like doing on projects on my own, but I enjoy programming most when I'm on a team of people working towards a common goal. Writing code that contributes to a larger system and seeing it merge with the contributions of others is some of the most satisfying times in development to me.

When I'm not programming, I'm probably hiking, tending to my ever-growing garden, playing games, making tools for D&D, or excitedly researching a subject completely irrelevant to me, like beekeeping or glassblowing.
