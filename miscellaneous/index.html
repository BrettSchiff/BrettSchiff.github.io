Ninsara

Ninsara is a D&D world which I'm working on. I am taking things out of the handbook I don't like and expanding on features I think there should be more detail on. I have documents in various states of completion that document towns, royal families, thieves guilds, magic items, and maps of the world. I'm almost done with an automatic character sheet that fills in details after picking race and class that makes the barrier for entry a lot lower.
